How It Works

From Buckets of Receipts to Zero
Manual Work — Without the Headache

Here's exactly how Venn fits into how your three crews operate day-to-day.

Corporate Cards & Receipt Flow
1
Employee makes a purchase Card functions as a Mastercard at any vendor — accepted everywhere, debit-style with your balance backing it.
2
Automated SMS receipt request sent Immediately after the transaction, your employee gets a text from Venn asking for the receipt. They just reply with a photo.
3
Receipt auto-matched, dashboard updated The receipt is matched to the transaction automatically. You see it in real time. No month-end pile of paper.
4
Non-compliant cards auto-locked Set a threshold — if an employee doesn't upload a receipt over $X, their card locks automatically until they do.
Vendor Payments (Free EFTs)
1
Add your vendor once Enter their banking details or invite them via email to securely submit their own — Venn verifies everything.
2
Set up the payment with approval Junior staff set up the EFT; it routes to you for approval before anything moves. Full audit trail.
3
Payment sent — free EFTs to Canadian vendors are free. ACH to the US, free. Wires to anywhere else, $6 flat. No surprises.

Loesen uses Saje for accounting. Venn exports transactions as CSV with GL codes — import directly into your accounting workflow, no manual data entry.
