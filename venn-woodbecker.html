How It Works

From 4-Day Delays to Same-Day Payments — Without the Manual Steps

Here's exactly how Venn fits into Woodbecker's payment workflow from the moment a PM submits to the moment your vendor is paid.

AP Payment Flow — Vendor Payments
›
1

PM Submits Payment

Junior PM selects vendor, attaches invoice, enters amount. Sent for approval — not processed yet.

›
2

You Approve

You review and approve in bulk or one-by-one. Set dollar thresholds — small payments can auto-approve.

›
3

Payment Sent Same Day

EFT or e-transfer leaves Venn immediately after approval — concrete contractors and trades get paid that day.

4

Export to Premier

Download a CSV or GL-coded export to reconcile in Premier Construction Software.

Corporate Cards — When You Need Them
1

Issue Cards Instantly

Physical or virtual cards for anyone on the team. Set limits: amount, merchant category (e.g. Home Depot only), or time period.

2

Restrict by Vendor

Lock cards to specific MCC codes — so a site card can only be used at hardware and supply vendors, not elsewhere.

3

Earn Cashback

Every card transaction earns cashback. The more you put on cards, the more you earn back — no cap.

ERP Integration — Premier Construction Software

✓
CSV / GL Code Export Export every transaction in a format compatible with Premier. No manual data entry — match to your chart of accounts.
✓
Native QuickBooks & Xero Integrations If you ever migrate ERPs, Venn connects natively to both — zero re-implementation needed.
✓
One Extra Account — Minimal Overhead Venn runs alongside Premier. You track it like any other bank account — one additional reconciliation line.
💡

No Minimum Balance. No Locked Funds.

Load only what you need for outgoing payments. Keep the rest in your existing bank. Venn doesn't require you to move your whole float — just what you're about to pay out.
