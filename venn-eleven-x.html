How It Works

From Manual Uploads and Broken Approvals
to a Single Platform That Runs Itself

Here's exactly how Venn fits into how eleven-x operates — cards, AP, and QuickBooks all connected.

Corporate Cards + QuickBooks
1
Issue cards to anyone instantly Virtual or physical Mastercard — no per-user fee. Give marketing their own cards with spend limits and category restrictions. No need to share manager's card.
2
Transactions sync to QuickBooks automatically Every card purchase appears in QuickBooks in real time — with GL code suggestions, merchant category, and receipt attached. No download, no upload, no 10-day delay.
3
Receipts collected by text — automatically Team member gets an SMS after every purchase. They reply with a photo — receipt matched and filed. Card locks after 3 missed receipts if you want to enforce it.
AP Approvals + Vendor Payments
1
Bills pulled from QuickBooks go through approval Unlike Float, bills synced from QuickBooks are routed through your approval rules — not skipped. Department heads approve their own bills before payment clears.
2
Department-level approval rules Set rules by department: marketing bills go to the head of marketing, finance bills go to finance. One approver per department — not a committee.
3
Pay vendors in CAD or USD from one account ~$35K USD + $20–30K CAD weekly. Free EFTs and ACH transfers. Approved bills marked as paid in QuickBooks automatically.
