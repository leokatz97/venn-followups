GEBIS Cardholder Onboarding Guide

Everything you need to accept your card, add it to your phone, and submit receipts — whether you're at the monastery, travelling, or at your desk.

1
Accept Your Card Invite

Your administrator will create a virtual card for you through the Venn dashboard — assigned to your department with a specific budget and validity period.

  1. You'll receive an email invite from Venn to join the GEBIS account
  2. Click the link to create your Venn account (takes ~2 minutes)
  3. Set your password and verify your email
  4. Once logged in, your card will be in the Cards section of the dashboard

Virtual cards are available instantly — you can start making purchases right away. Physical cards arrive by mail in 5–7 business days.

2
Add Your Card to Apple Pay or Google Pay

Add your Venn Mastercard to your phone's wallet to tap and pay in-store, in-app, and online. Works with both virtual and physical cards.

 Apple Pay (iPhone)
  1. Open the Wallet app on your iPhone
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top right
  3. Select "Debit or Credit Card"
  4. Enter your Venn card details manually or scan with your camera
  5. Review and tap Continue
  6. Tap "Agree" to accept terms
  7. Choose verification method: Email or SMS
  8. Enter the 6-digit one-time passcode you receive
  9. Done — your card is now in Apple Pay
▶ Google Pay (Android)
  1. Download Google Wallet from the Play Store
  2. Open Google Wallet and tap the "+" button
  3. Select "Payment card" → "New credit or debit card"
  4. Enter your Venn card details manually or scan
  5. Tap "Accept" to agree to terms
  6. Choose verification method: Email or SMS
  7. Enter the 6-digit one-time passcode you receive
  8. Done — your card is now in Google Pay

The verification passcode is sent to your own email or phone number, not the admin's.

3
Using Your Card

Your Venn Mastercard works everywhere Mastercard is accepted — online, in-store, and internationally.

  • ✅ Tap to pay in-store using Apple Pay or Google Pay
  • ✅ Online purchases — use your card number, expiry, and CVV from the Venn dashboard
  • ✅ Multi-currency — your card automatically selects CAD or USD to avoid unnecessary conversions
  • ✅ Check your balance — log in anytime to see your remaining budget and transaction history

Spending limits: Your admin sets a budget on each card. If a purchase is declined, it likely means you've reached your card's limit — check your balance in the dashboard or contact your admin.

4
Upload Receipts

Every time you make a purchase, you'll be asked for a receipt. Pick whichever method is easiest for you.

On Your Phone
Reply to SMS

You'll get a text message after each purchase. Tap the link, snap a photo of the receipt, and submit. Takes 10 seconds.

On Your Phone or Computer
Reply to Email

You'll also get an email notification. Reply with the receipt image attached and it's automatically matched to the transaction.

On Your Phone
Snap in the Venn App

Open Venn → go to Tasks → tap the transaction → take a photo. Venn's AI extracts the details automatically.

On Your Computer
Upload on the Web

Log in at app.venn.ca → go to Tasks → find the transaction → upload the receipt file. Or use Auto Match to let Venn match it for you.

Tip: Save the Venn receipt number in your phone as "Venn Receipts" so you always know where texts are coming from. Upload receipts right away — it takes seconds and saves everyone time at month-end.

Note for GEBIS team: Receipt uploads are important for your accounting team's records and QuickBooks sync. If you make 5 purchases without uploading a receipt, your card may be temporarily locked until receipts are submitted.
