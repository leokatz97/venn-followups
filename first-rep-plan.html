Growth

Referral Program

Simple, automatic, costs nothing. Runs entirely through Shopify's discount code system — no manual tracking.

How It Works

Step 1

Client finishes their first session. You send them a unique code (e.g. FIRSTREPMIKE).

Step 2

They share it. Their friend gets $10 off their first session at checkout.

Step 3

Friend books → original client gets their next session free.

"If you know anyone trying to break into sales, send them your code — [CODE]. They get $10 off and you get your next one free."
Marketing

How to Get Clients

LinkedIn

One honest launch post. Lean into your story. Then one post a week — one thing you wish you knew before your first sales job. That's your whole strategy.

Facebook Groups

Ontario new grad and alumni groups. Add value first, answer a question or two, then mention what you offer.

Word of Mouth

Tell five people personally — not a mass message. At 5–10 clients a month this alone is enough.

Referral Codes

Every client becomes a distribution channel. One hire leads to three more conversations.

Hook with questions, not statements — "Struggling to get your first job in sales?" "Not getting callbacks after interviews?" "Not sure how to stand out on LinkedIn?"

Payments

How to Launch on Shopify

Shopify is the cleanest way to accept payments without building anything custom. Start on the Starter plan at $5/month — shareable payment links, no full store needed.

  1. Go to shopify.com and create an account under "First Rep"
  2. Create three products: Intro Call (Free), 30-Minute Session — $50, 3-Session Package — $120
  3. Connect Shopify Payments to accept credit cards — no third-party processor needed
  4. Install Sesami or Appointo (free booking apps) so clients pick a time slot right at checkout — no separate Calendly needed
  5. Set up automatic order confirmation emails so clients get a booking link the moment they pay
  6. For referral codes: Discounts → Create Discount → unique code per client → $10 off → Shopify logs every use automatically
  7. Share product links on LinkedIn and in a Linktree — no website required to start
