How It Works for Miranu
From Card Blocks and Manual Receipts
to Controlled, Isolated Payments — Automatically
Set it up once. Venn handles the rest.
Virtual Cards — Isolate Every Vendor
1
Create a card per vendor
Assign one virtual card to your Bay Street workspace, another to each software subscription. Each gets its own card number and spending limit.
2
Set a limit that matches your contract
Cap the card at the exact amount you expect to be charged. Any attempt above that limit is automatically declined — no manual monitoring needed.
3
Lock, delete, or adjust in real time
If a vendor triggers issues again, lock that single card from the app in seconds. Everything else stays live. No new card reissuance, no bank calls.
Receipt Capture — No More Year-End Hunts
1
Spend with your Venn card
Marko or Jelena uses a physical or virtual Venn card. The transaction logs instantly in the platform.
2
Get a receipt prompt by text or email
Venn sends an automatic prompt right after the purchase. Reply with a photo or screenshot of the receipt — done in 10 seconds.
3
Run the missing-receipts report at year-end
Filter by "transactions not containing receipts" and see exactly what's outstanding — by person, by date, by amount. No spreadsheet required.
