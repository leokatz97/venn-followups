Bonus: Interest on Balances

Venn pays ~2% APY on funds held in your account. Unlike a traditional bank where operating funds sit idle, every dollar in Venn earns interest until the moment it's spent.

What about the credit float? Venn is prepaid — your funds need to be loaded before they're spent. But because your balance earns 2% APY while it sits in Venn, your idle cash is always working for you. No more lending your bank interest-free float for 20 days every cycle. The cashback uplift alone more than offsets any cost-of-capital difference — and the interest on top is pure upside.
