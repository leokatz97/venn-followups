Hidden Cost: Credit Float

Your current card gives you a ~20-day credit float — but that means your bank holds your cash interest-free for 20 days every billing cycle. At a conservative 5% cost of capital, here's what that float actually costs you:

Monthly spend Annual float cost
$250K / mo$8,219

Formula: annual spend × (20 ÷ 365) × 5% cost of capital. With Venn, your funds earn interest until the moment they're spent.
