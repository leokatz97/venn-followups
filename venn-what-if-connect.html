How It Works

From TD Wires to Instant Payments —
Without the Manual Steps

Sending a Payment (Wire or ACH)
1
Add Vendor
Set up the payee once — name, bank details, country. Venn supports 180 countries, 36 currencies.
2
Choose Method
ACH for US vendors (free), wire for international ($6 flat), EFT for Canadian companies ($0).
3
Send & Confirm
Approve and send directly from your Venn balance. No branch visit, no manual wire form.
4
Auto-Export
Transaction pushed to QuickBooks automatically — accountant sees it in real time.
Corporate Cards + Expense Management
1
Card Issued
Physical + virtual cards issued per team member. Spend limit = your Venn balance. Accepted like credit, cashback earned.
2
Purchase Made
Employee spends. Receipt forwarded or snapped in-app. Card locking available if needed.
3
Auto-Matched
Transaction matched to receipt and GL code. No manual reconciliation by your accountant.
QuickBooks Integration
Your accountant already uses QuickBooks to manage your TD transactions. With Venn, the workflow stays the same — they get their own Venn login, transactions push to QBO throughout the month, or they pull a statement at month-end. No re-learning required.
