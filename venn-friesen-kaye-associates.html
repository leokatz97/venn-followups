From Personal Cards and Manual Receipts to Automated Expense Management

Here's exactly how Venn fits into your workflow — from issuing cards to syncing with QuickBooks.

Corporate Cards — How It Works
1
Issue a card to your instructor in seconds

Create a virtual card in Venn, assign it to your instructor, set a monthly limit (e.g. $2,500), and restrict it to travel categories like hotels and flights. Physical card ships in 3 business days and loads into Apple or Google Wallet instantly.

2
Instructor pays — no personal card, no reimbursement

They tap their Venn card or use the virtual card number. Transactions are charged against the company balance in real time. They only see their own card and transactions — nothing else.

3
Transactions sync to QuickBooks automatically

Every purchase flows directly into QuickBooks through Venn's native integration. No manual entry, no receipt chasing. Your account manager reviews categorized transactions — not individual receipts.

QuickBooks Integration
🔗
Native QuickBooks sync — already included in Plus

Connect your QuickBooks account once. All Venn transactions — cards, transfers, and payments — sync automatically with GL codes, merchant names, and categories. No CSV exports, no manual reconciliation.

💳
1% cashback on all card spend — automatically applied

Every dollar your instructors spend on Venn cards earns 1% back. No foreign transaction fees, no FX markup. If they're ever spending in USD (like in Texas), the rate is 0.25% — far below any bank or card provider.

The bottom line for Friesen Kaye & Associates: Your account manager goes from manually tracking personal card receipts and processing reimbursements to simply reviewing pre-categorized transactions in QuickBooks. One $40/mo flat fee. Unlimited cards. Expenses handled.
