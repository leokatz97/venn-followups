USD Accumulation Strategy

Rather than wiring USD back to your local bank after every payment cycle, many customers let their USD balance sit in Venn and earn 2% interest. When it's time to repatriate funds, you wire it back in bulk (one $6 wire) rather than paying per transaction. For a business processing your volume of China supplier payments and CBP duties, this approach alone typically saves $200–$400/month in unnecessary wire fees.
